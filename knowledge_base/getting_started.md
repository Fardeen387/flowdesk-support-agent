# FlowDesk — Getting Started Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Workspace

When you sign up for FlowDesk, you'll be prompted to create a **Workspace**. A workspace is your team's home — it contains all your projects, members, and settings.

- Choose a workspace name (usually your company or team name)
- Upload a workspace logo (optional)
- Select your team size
- Select your primary use case (Software Development, Marketing, Operations, etc.)

You can change all of these later in Settings → Workspace.

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Project

Click the **"New Project"** button in the left sidebar.

**Project settings:**
- **Name** — Give your project a clear, descriptive name
- **Description** — Optional but recommended
- **Visibility** — Public (all workspace members) or Private (invite only)
- **Default View** — Choose between List, Board (Kanban), or Timeline (Gantt)
- **Color & Icon** — For easy visual identification

Click "Create Project" to proceed.

---

## Step 3: Add Tasks

Inside your project, click **"Add Task"** or press `T` as a keyboard shortcut.

**Each task can have:**
- Title
- Description (supports markdown formatting)
- Assignee (one or multiple team members)
- Due date
- Priority (Urgent, High, Medium, Low)
- Status (To Do, In Progress, In Review, Done — customizable)
- Labels/Tags
- Subtasks
- File attachments
- Comments

---

## Step 4: Organize with Sections

Group tasks into **Sections** within a project. For example:
- Backlog
- Sprint 1
- Sprint 2
- Completed

To add a section, click **"Add Section"** at the bottom of your task list or between existing sections.

---

## Step 5: Invite Your Team

Go to **Settings → Team → Invite Members**.

Enter your teammates' email addresses. They will receive an invitation email. Once they accept, they'll appear in your workspace and can be assigned to tasks and projects.

You can assign roles during the invite:
- **Admin** — Full workspace access
- **Member** — Standard access
- **Guest** — Limited to specific projects

---

## Step 6: Set Up Notifications

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to configure how you want to be notified:

- Email notifications (task assigned, due date, mentions)
- In-app notifications
- Slack notifications (if Slack is connected)
- Mobile push notifications

We recommend enabling notifications for: task assignments, @mentions, and due date reminders (24 hours before).

---

## Step 7: Connect Your Tools

Go to **Settings → Integrations** to connect FlowDesk with your existing tools:

- **Slack** — Get task updates in your Slack channels
- **GitHub/GitLab** — Link commits and PRs to tasks
- **Google Drive** — Attach files directly from Drive
- **Zoom** — Create Zoom meeting links from within tasks
