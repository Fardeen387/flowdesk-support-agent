# FlowDesk — Pricing Plans

## Overview

FlowDesk offers three pricing tiers: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. All plans include a 14-day free trial of Pro features with no credit card required.

---

## Free Plan — $0/month

Best for individuals and small teams just getting started.

**Included features:**
- Up to 5 team members
- Up to 10 active projects
- 5 GB file storage
- Basic task management (tasks, subtasks, due dates)
- List and Kanban board views
- Mobile app access
- Community support (forums only)

**Limitations:**
- No Gantt chart view
- No time tracking
- No custom fields
- No API access
- No priority support
- Integrations limited to Google Drive only

---

## Pro Plan — $12/user/month (billed annually) or $15/user/month (billed monthly)

Best for growing teams that need advanced features and integrations.

**Everything in Free, plus:**
- Up to 50 team members
- Unlimited active projects
- 100 GB file storage
- Gantt chart and Timeline views
- Time tracking and reporting
- Custom fields (text, number, date, dropdown)
- All integrations (Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Zoom, etc.)
- REST API and GraphQL API access
- Advanced permissions and role management
- Automated workflows (up to 100 automations/month)
- Priority email support (response within 24 hours)
- Export to CSV and PDF

**Pro Plan Billing:**
- Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly
- You are billed per active user (invited members who accepted the invite)
- Pending invites do not count toward billing

---

## Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing

Best for large organizations needing security, compliance, and dedicated support.

**Everything in Pro, plus:**
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited file storage
- SSO (Single Sign-On) via SAML 2.0
- SCIM provisioning for automated user management
- Advanced audit logs (90-day retention)
- Custom data retention policies
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- Custom contract and invoicing
- On-premise deployment option
- Unlimited automations
- Priority phone and video support

**To get Enterprise pricing:**
Contact our sales team at sales@flowdesk.io or book a demo at flowdesk.io/enterprise.

## Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Settings → Billing. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**What happens to my data if I downgrade from Pro to Free?**
Your data is preserved. However, features that exceed Free plan limits will be locked (not deleted). For example, if you have 15 projects, all 15 remain but only 10 will be active. You can choose which 10 to keep active.

**Is there a discount for nonprofits or educational institutions?**
Yes. FlowDesk offers a 50% discount for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact support@flowdesk.io with your organization's verification documents.

**Do you offer a refund?**
Yes. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, contact support@flowdesk.io for a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are prorated for the unused portion of the billing period.

**Are there any setup fees?**
No. There are no setup fees for any plan including Enterprise.
