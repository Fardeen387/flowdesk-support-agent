# FlowDesk — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**What is FlowDesk?**
FlowDesk is a cloud-based project management SaaS that helps teams plan, track, and collaborate on work in one place. It supports task management, team collaboration, file sharing, time tracking, and project reporting.

**Who is FlowDesk for?**
FlowDesk is designed for teams of all sizes — from solo freelancers to enterprise teams with 500+ members. It works well for software teams, marketing teams, design agencies, and operations teams.

**Is FlowDesk available on mobile?**
Yes. FlowDesk has native iOS and Android apps available on the App Store and Google Play Store. All features available on the web are also available on mobile.

**What languages does FlowDesk support?**
FlowDesk currently supports English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Japanese. More languages are being added quarterly.

**Is my data secure on FlowDesk?**
Yes. FlowDesk is SOC 2 Type II certified and uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All data is backed up daily and stored in redundant servers.

## Account & Access

**How do I create a FlowDesk account?**
Go to flowdesk.io and click "Get Started Free". Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.

**How do I reset my password?**
On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and you'll receive a password reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes.

**How do I change my email address?**
Go to Settings → Profile → Contact Information. Click "Edit" next to your email address, enter the new email, and verify it via the confirmation link sent to the new address.

**Can I have multiple accounts with the same email?**
No. Each email address can only be associated with one FlowDesk account. If you need separate workspaces, use the multi-workspace feature within a single account.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone → Delete Account. Note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your projects, tasks, and data will be permanently removed after 30 days.

## Team & Collaboration

**How do I invite team members?**
Go to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter the email addresses of your teammates (comma-separated for multiple invites). They'll receive an invitation email with a link to join your workspace.

**How many team members can I invite?**
- Free plan: up to 5 members
- Pro plan: up to 50 members
- Enterprise plan: unlimited members

**Can I set different roles for team members?**
Yes. FlowDesk supports four roles: Owner, Admin, Member, and Guest. Owners have full access. Admins can manage members and settings. Members can create and edit projects. Guests have read-only access to specific projects they are invited to.

**How do I remove a team member?**
Go to Settings → Team, find the member, click the three-dot menu next to their name, and select "Remove from Workspace". Their access is revoked immediately.

**Can external clients view my projects?**
Yes. You can invite external users as Guests. Guests can only see projects they are explicitly added to and cannot access workspace-level settings or other projects.

## Integrations

**Does FlowDesk integrate with Slack?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack and connect your Slack workspace. You can configure notifications for task assignments, due date reminders, and project updates to be sent to specific Slack channels.

**Does FlowDesk integrate with GitHub?**
Yes. The GitHub integration lets you link pull requests and commits to FlowDesk tasks. Go to Settings → Integrations → GitHub to connect your repositories.

**Does FlowDesk integrate with Google Drive?**
Yes. You can attach Google Drive files directly to tasks and projects. Go to Settings → Integrations → Google Drive and authorize access.

**What other integrations are available?**
FlowDesk integrates with: Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Google Drive, Dropbox, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Jira, Zapier, and Webhooks for custom integrations.

**Does FlowDesk have an API?**
Yes. FlowDesk offers a REST API and a GraphQL API. API documentation is available at docs.flowdesk.io/api. API access is available on Pro and Enterprise plans only.
