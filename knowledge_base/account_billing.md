# FlowDesk — Account & Billing Guide

## Managing Your Account

### Profile Settings
Go to **Settings → Profile** to update:
- Full name and display name
- Profile photo
- Job title and department
- Timezone (important for due date reminders and scheduling)
- Language preference

### Changing Your Password
Go to Settings → Security → Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password before setting a new one. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include one uppercase letter and one number.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security. To enable it:
1. Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

2FA is mandatory for Enterprise plan workspaces.

### Connected Accounts
You can link your Google or GitHub account to enable one-click login. Go to Settings → Security → Connected Accounts. Once linked, you can log in using either your email/password or the connected account.

## Workspace Settings

### Renaming Your Workspace
Go to Settings → Workspace → General. Update the workspace name and click Save. The new name appears immediately across all team members' accounts.

### Workspace Timezone
Setting the correct workspace timezone ensures that due dates, reminders, and reports reflect your team's local time. Go to Settings → Workspace → General → Timezone.

### Transferring Workspace Ownership
Only the current Owner can transfer ownership. Go to Settings → Workspace → Ownership → Transfer Ownership. Select a new owner from your team members list. You will be downgraded to Admin after the transfer.

### Deleting a Workspace
Go to Settings → Workspace → Danger Zone → Delete Workspace. This permanently deletes all projects, tasks, files, and member data. This action cannot be undone. You must be the workspace Owner to delete it.

## Billing & Invoices

### Viewing Your Current Plan
Go to Settings → Billing. You'll see your current plan, next billing date, number of active seats, and monthly/annual cost.

### Upgrading to Pro
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Upgrade to Pro
2. Enter your payment details (credit/debit card or PayPal)
3. Choose monthly or annual billing (annual saves 20%)
4. Click "Confirm Upgrade"

Your Pro features activate immediately after payment.

### Downloading Invoices
Go to Settings → Billing → Invoice History. All past invoices are listed with the date, amount, and a download link. Invoices are in PDF format and include your company name and billing address if configured.

### Updating Billing Information
Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Method to update your card details. Go to Settings → Billing → Billing Address to update your company name, address, and VAT/tax ID for invoice purposes.

### Annual vs Monthly Billing
- **Annual billing:** Pay upfront for 12 months, save 20% compared to monthly
- **Monthly billing:** Pay month-to-month, cancel anytime with no penalty
- You can switch from monthly to annual at any time — the remaining monthly days are prorated and credited toward your annual plan

## Team Seat Management

### How Seats Are Counted
You are billed per **active seat** — a user who has accepted their workspace invitation. Pending invitations do not count as billable seats.

### Adding Seats
When you invite a new member on the Pro plan, a new seat is automatically added to your subscription. The cost for the new seat is prorated for the remaining days in your billing cycle.

### Removing Seats
When you remove a team member, their seat is freed up. The credit for the unused portion of their seat is applied to your next invoice automatically.

### Seat Limits by Plan
- Free: 5 seats maximum
- Pro: 50 seats maximum
- Enterprise: Unlimited seats

## Data & Privacy

### Exporting Your Data
You can export all your workspace data at any time. Go to Settings → Workspace → Export Data. You'll receive a download link via email within 24 hours containing all your projects, tasks, comments, and files in JSON and CSV format.

### Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained indefinitely while your account is active
- Deleted tasks: Retained in Trash for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- Deleted account data: Permanently deleted after 30 days from account deletion
- Enterprise plans can configure custom data retention policies

### GDPR Compliance
FlowDesk is fully GDPR compliant. You can submit a data access request or data deletion request by emailing privacy@flowdesk.io. Requests are processed within 30 days.
